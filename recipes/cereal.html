Cereal!

Description

Its cereal! Not much I can say about it honestly. I just like it. I am lazy and I do not like other breakfast foods.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Fork
  • Couch
  • Tv

Steps

  1. Pour milk into a bowl.
  2. Pour cereal into a bowl. (Different bowl)
  3. Sit on couch.
  4. Use tv remote (I forgot to mention this in ingredients section, my apologies) and turn on cartoons
  5. Pour a little bit of cereal into bowl of milk
  6. Eat and watch cartoons until you eat all cereal in milk bowl.
  7. Repeat steps 5-7 until cereal bowl is empty.
  8. Get more cereal.
